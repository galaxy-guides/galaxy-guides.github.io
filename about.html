Galaxy Guides brings an experienced and knowledgable astronomer with a telescope to your private event!

We help bring the wonders of the universe right to you through guided telescope tours, science communication, and sharing the mythology of the night sky.

Dazzle your friends and family with up close views of the planets within our Solar System, stars clusters, planetary nebulae, galaxies, and more!

Ask your Galaxy Guide about the science behind these objects and about the history of humans from all different cultures observing the night sky from pre-history to the modern day.
