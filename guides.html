Meet Our Guides

Jack Lubin

Founder and Guide

Jack received his PhD in astrophysics from UC Irvine in 2023, specializing in the discovery and characterization of exoplanets as an observational astronomer. You can read more about his research and hobbies at his website . He is passionate about bringing astronomy to everyone and enjoys science communication as much as research. He participated in many outreach programs and observing runs while in grad school, details include:

  • 51 nights observing experience with Keck 10-meter telescopes in Hawaii
  • 32 nights observing experience with Shane 3-meter telescope at Lick Observatory in San Jose
  • 2 Astro on Tap public talks
  • Instructor for Intro2Astro free online astro research summer program
  • Writer for Astrobites blog
  • An invited talk at iTelescope Webinars
  • A finalist at UCI's Grad Slam 2023
  • Teaching assistant for 5 years at UCI, teaching everything from Physics 101 to advanced astrophysics courses
  • 1 year as a physics and astronomy tutor for Vanderbilt Athletics
  • Test writer for the Orange County Science Olympiad's Astronomy test for 6th graders
  • Helping pre-schoolers with their question "Where does the Sun go at night?"
