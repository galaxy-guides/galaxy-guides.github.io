Explore our services

Core Services

Star-Gazing

Our flagship service! A Galaxy Guide will bring a high power telescope and their knowledge of the night sky to your event. We set everything up and find the most interesting objects in the sky. You and your guests can take turns looking through the eye-piece to get an up close view of many important and famous astronomical objects. All the while, your Galaxy Guide will describe the science, history, and mythology associated with the object you are looking at. Audience questions are always more than welcomed, they are encouraged!

What you get:

  • A Galaxy Guide
  • A high power telescope
  • Q&A with your Galaxy Guide

Science Talk

Is there a topic within astronomy that you would love to know more about? Have a Galaxy Guide put on a scientific talk for you and your guests! We have a variety of talk topics ready to go for you to chose from or we can design a custom talk specifically for your event. Our guides are well practiced in speaking to audiences and can tailor the talk to the background level of your guests. We encourage Q&A!

Talk topics include:

  • Exoplanets: Finding Earth 2.0
  • Great Measurements: How did we discover what we know?
  • Fermi's Paradox: Are we alone?
  • The Big Bang to Now: How the Universe came to be

Add On Services

Astrophotography

In addition to star-gazing, we bring the equipment and knowledge required for taking beautiful images of the objects that you can see through the telescope. Afterall, a picture is worth a thousand words! We will help you take photographs of the night sky and then we print them on the spot. A fantastic party favor for your guests!

  • A Galaxy Guide
  • A high power telescope
  • Q&A with your Galaxy Guide
  • A DSLR camera and telescope attachments
  • A portable printer to create peel and stick images instantly

Live Stream

Have a large crowd at your event and want to avoid long lines to view through the telescope? We will set up a live-stream connecting our high power telescopes to a TV so that many can enjoy the views at once.

Giveaways

Give your guests a party favor they won't forget! We bring along all kinds of fun astronomy-themed giveaway items, including: astronomy stickers, pins, books, solar viewing glasses, professional astro photos, and more! Great for events with children of all ages.
